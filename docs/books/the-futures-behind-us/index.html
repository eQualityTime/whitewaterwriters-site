The Future's Behind Us

After being sent away for the summer by their parents, a friendship forms between a group of 5 teenagers who very quickly have to learn to put their full trust into each other. A story unfolds where we learn the quirks and weaknesses of each character. As the group are exploring, they stumble across a time machine which takes them to the future, filled with both a waste land and an beautiful land in the sky, flipping their lives upside down. A romance blossoms, and drama occurs. Will the group escape capture and will the robot and boy live happily ever after?
