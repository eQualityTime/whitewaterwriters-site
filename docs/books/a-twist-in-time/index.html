A Twist in Time

They were pursued. Hunted. Their advance was imminent. They had gone so far, that they could not afford to be held captive now. Paris. The Eiffel Tower. They needed to get there, and quick. Otherwise, they would be doomed for eternity… Johni Stein, an exceptionally talented 12 year old, is a student of a government academy for young scientists. At AFESTY (Academy For Exceptionally Scientifically Talented Youth) HQ, he receives a scruffily written letter, from a man called Andrew Shepardson. Shepardson had allegedly created a time travel device with limitless possibilities. He is sent to prove the validity of these lunatic claims, but an accident nearly results in his demise…
