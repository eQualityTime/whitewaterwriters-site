Cloud Attack

Do you like like the sound of conflict? Well then here’s the book for you. Stormzy is a cloud who is 14 and takes on a very important role when a disaster strikes; A war between humans and clouds. Stormzy and his friends try to put it right.... But will it help? Could it destroy Cloudville? Want to find out? Then you’d better open the book now! This book portrays a very important message and I think you will enjoy the book so don't just stand there reading the blurb open the front cover and start reading!
