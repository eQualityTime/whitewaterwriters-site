In the Shadows

‘Cold. Rough. Hard. I am sat here on the floor of a small confined space with no light. I do not know where I am, this is not my bedroom. Why am I here?’ In a dark contemporary setting of Stoke-on-Trent, two teenage boys and five teenage girls embark on a series of quests in hope of finding a missing six year old girl who desperately wants to be back in the care of her foster parents. Will this group of determined teenagers ever find her and those responsible?
