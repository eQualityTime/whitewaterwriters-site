Careers

As a publisher we employ various staff to manage talent development, produce cover art, maintain our software and manage our relationship with retailers like Amazon.

All of these roles are remote, with the exception of talent development, which is an intense, on-site process. We do not accept unsolicited applications for talent development. Cover artists are considered on a rolling basis, please apply by social media after reading this.
