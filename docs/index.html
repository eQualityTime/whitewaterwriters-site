White Water Writers has been an independent publisher of YA fiction since 2009. We focus on talent development, working with groups of young authors to unlock their potential and confidence.

We work with schools and universities, taking students though an intensive week-long course in creative writing using the TooManyCooks method. Groups start with a blank page on Monday morning, are lead through a series of exercises and activities and publish their own collaboratively written novel on Friday afternoon.

Nobody outside the group touches a key during the week - everything is entirely the work of the students involved.
