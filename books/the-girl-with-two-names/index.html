The Girl with Two Names

There is trouble in Stoke-on-Trent… They’re just normal teenagers , living a normal life , until the unthinkable happens in their small town. People are going missing , which is causing chaos throughout the country. No one knows who , what or how these disappearances are happening. The mystery reeks with possibilities and high levels of danger. With the Government left in panic , it is up to the four teens to stop the disappearances and save the city. Can they stop it in time? Four friends One black book And a girl with two names…
