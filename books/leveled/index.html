Leveled

Alex and his mother live in poverty, in a small flat barely big enough for either of them. Alex’s mother works as a cleaner and takes the night shift, so they don’t often see one another. So when Alex hears of a virtual reality game called: A New Reality that promises great riches to the winner, he spends all his savings on a chip that gets him into the game.When Alex enters the game, he’s faced with new friends, foes, and difficult decisions he may not be able to make alone. But is money more important than friendship?
