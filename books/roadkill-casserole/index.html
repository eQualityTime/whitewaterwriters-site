Roadkill casserole

When Sophie and her friends go to London for a long weekend of shopping it’s nothing like they first expected. This weekend started out as a fun trip but soon turned sour with an ex-boyfriend stalking them, a dog catcher hating them and Nan serving the most unusual dinner. Plus a couple of twists along the way but that’s not the worst part, not by far - the whole group gets caught up in a thrilling murder mystery which results in Sophie calling in help from her brother Rat. Will he find out who done it and why?
