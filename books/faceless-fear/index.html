Faceless Fear

Everyone has many fears! From little baby spiders to thunder. But nothing is more powerful than a child’s Fear. Eliza, a girl with a tough childhood, her biggest fear is being silenced by her controlling parents. Children thrive on being heard and being acknowledged but when a child has no voice, they become distant and lonely: their pain makes Fear powerful! Fear uses everyone’s fear to take over the human world, but when a group of people with many different fears realise what's going on, they decide to take matters into their own hands and save everyone from their own fears. Will they be able to overpower the most powerful emotion or will Fear take over everyone’s life?
