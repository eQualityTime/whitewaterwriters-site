The Adventures of Chris Wheeler

Chris Wheeler is no ordinary 15-year-old. He is a world explorer. In this collection of short stories for children, you can read about his fantastic adventures which take him to a mysterious island, a magic school, the heat of the desert and even to another planet! Will Chris defeat the villains? Can he make it home? Will he be lost forever? Read on to find out more.
