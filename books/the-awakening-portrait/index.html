The Awakening Portrait

Lilli is an astronaut. She has just returned from the international space station when she then discovers that all the children had disappeared of the face of the earth. People start to forget that children even existed. It's chaos, children are disappearing left right and centre. What’s going on!? Now the adults are disappearing too, Lilli must save everyone . This is all happening because of the evil Veronica . Will Lilli be able to save the human race from extinction or will everyone disappear….
