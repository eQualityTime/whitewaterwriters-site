The Disaster at Ryde Orphanage

In the orphanage, no one is safe... James Prince, the fourteen-year-old living in an orphanage for boys is the hero of the story while Maggie is the owner of the orphanage and the mastermind of a truly evil plot. Only James, with the help of his best friend, Teddy, and detective Mark Wright can save the lives of many adults. Their fate lies in his hands. Can James and Mark save the day after the disaster at Ryde Orphanage for Boys, or will they be caught in the act?
